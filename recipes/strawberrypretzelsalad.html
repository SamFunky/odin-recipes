Strawberry Pretzel Salad

Description

This salty, creamy, crunchy dessert is a staple holiday food in the Midwest.

Ingredients

  • Cool Whip
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Strawberry Jello
  • Water
  • Pretzels
  • Cream Cheese
  • Strawberries

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 F
  2. Prepare Jell-O as per package directions
  3. Mix together crushed pretzels, butter, and sugar
  4. Press into a 9 x 13 pan
  5. Bake for 10 minutes and let cool completely
  6. Beat cream cheese with sugar until smooth
  7. Fold in the Cool Whip
  8. Spread cream cheese mixture over the pretzel crust
  9. Refrigerate for 30 minutes
  10. Slice strawberries and arrange on top of cream cheese layer
  11. Slowly pour the Jell-O mixture over strawberries
  12. Refrogerate until set and enjoy
