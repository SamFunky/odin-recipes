Fruit Salad

Description

This sweet and sour dish is sure to please at any summer event.

Ingredients

  • Red Grapes
  • Pineapple
  • Strawberry
  • Blueberry
  • Blackberry
  • Kiwi
  • Lime Zest
  • Lime Juice
  • Honey

Steps

  1. Wash and dry fruits well
  2. Peel, core, and chop the pineapple
  3. Slice the strawberries
  4. Peel and chop the kiwi
  5. Add the fruits to a large serving bowl
  6. In a small bowl, whisk together lime zest, lime juice, and honey
  7. Pour lime and homey mixture over the fruit and enjoy
