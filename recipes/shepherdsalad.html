Shepherd Salad

Description

This dish is a crowd favorite at many Turkish festivals and celebrations.

Ingredients

  • Roma Tomatoes
  • Cucumbers
  • Parsley
  • Black Olives
  • Red Onion
  • Cider Vinegar
  • Lemon Juice
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Greek Feta Cheese

Steps

  1. Seed and chop 3 Roma tomatoes and 2 cucumbers
  2. Pick the leaves of 1 bunch of parsley
  3. Pit and half one half cup of black olives
  4. Chop half a small red onion
  5. Combine 2 tbsp of cider vinegar, 2 tbsp of lemon juice, and 4 tbsp of olive oil
  6. Dice 8 ounces of Greek feta cheese
  7. Combine all ingredients except for the feta cheese
  8. Let sit at room temperature for 1 hour
  9. Add in feta cheese and stir
